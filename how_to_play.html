How to play

             Here is an opportunity to test your basics. The game is simple, you have to select the correct operand and opertator within the given time (90 seconds) . For those of you who don't know what operators and operands are, not to worry. The blue bubbles on your left are operands and the orange bubbles on your right are operators.

             Using the operands and operators on the screen , you have to arrive at the number in the blue box at the right bottom of your screen to proceed to the next level. You can try, try and try till you succeed but remember the time is ticking!!! With every level you clear, you earn a point that gets added to your "SCORE" that can be seen at the bottom of your screen.
